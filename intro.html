Introduction to Artificial Intelligence

Logical theorists and symbolic reasoning systems

In the 1950s and early 1960s, a number of mathematicians and logicians began to explore how mathematics and logic could be used to model human thinking and reasoning. Notable logical theorists include Alan Turing and John von Neumann. They came up with some concepts about intelligent machines and autonomous intelligent systems, and although computer technology was not yet sufficient to realize these ideas, these ideas laid the foundation for the later development of AI.

Early expert systems

In the late 1960s and early 1970s, people began to try to build systems that could simulate the knowledge and reasoning process of human experts, which was the early expert system. These systems are based on rules and logic, using human-written rules to simulate the decision-making process of experts. Although these systems have achieved some success in some specific areas, they have a narrow range of applications due to the limitations of computer performance and knowledge representation.

The rise of connectionism

In the 1980s, connectionism began to rise. Connectionism is a model inspired by biological neural networks that simulates the connections and information transfer between neurons in the human brain. With this model, people began to explore how to make computers learn from data, rather than relying on human-written rules. This laid the foundation for machine learning and deep learning.

Advances in machine learning

Since the 1990s, machine learning has gradually become one of the mainstream technologies in the field of AI. With the massive growth of data and the improvement of computing power, machine learning algorithms began to make significant progress, including support vector machines, decision trees, neural networks, and so on. These algorithms are widely used in image recognition, speech recognition, natural language processing and so on.

Rise of Deep learning

At the beginning of the 21st century, with the rise of deep learning technology, the field of AI made a huge breakthrough. The deep learning model mimics the neural network structure of human brain, learns abstract features of data through multi-level neural network, and achieves amazing results in image recognition, speech recognition, natural language processing and so on.
